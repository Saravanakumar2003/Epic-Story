Yo, folks! I'm Saravanakumar Ramaswamy, born and bred in the heart of Chennai, India, on 10th October 2003. My awesome squad includes my rockstar parents, Ramaswamy and Alamelu, and my elder bro, Kannappan Ramaswamy. School, coding, geeking out about nuclear stuff, and lending a hand wherever I can—that's my jam! But you know what? Beyond all that, I'm a knowledge junkie and a big-time dreamer. This is the epic saga of how I evolved into the dude I am today, packed with wild tales and epic lessons. So, buckle up, peeps! Let's dive deep into my past and shoot for the stars in my future. And hey, remember: The best is yet to come!

SchoolDays

Back in the day, I was a student at Sethu Bhaskara Matric Hr Sec School, from 1st to 12th grade.

Now, picture this: I was the quintessential introvert, scared to even whisper in public. Events? Nah, not my scene. But then came the Corona Pandemic, hitting us hard during my final year in 12th grade. Something clicked in me—I decided to break out of my shell and try new things. And you know what? I started throwing myself into events left, right, and center.

One fine day in January 2021, I dipped my toes into a competition organized by the National Children Science Congress. To my shock and delight, I snagged the district-level win and got the green light for the state-level showdown. Ecstatic, I poured my heart and soul into prepping for the big day. I knew my project inside out, every nook and cranny.

But when the moment arrived, nerves got the best of me. I stumbled and bumbled through my presentation like a lost duckling. Needless to say, I didn't make the cut for the national level. Was I bummed about losing? Nah. I was crushed because I couldn't do justice to my project. Plus, seeing the disappointment in my teachers' eyes stung real bad—they had my back all along, rooting for me.

That's when I made a vow: to level up my public speaking game. I dove headfirst into more events, determined to turn my weakness into a strength. And you know what they say: practice makes perfect!

College Days

Transitioning from the familiar halls of Sethu Bhaskara Matric Hr Sec School to the dynamic environment of Velammal Engineering College marked a significant milestone in my journey. It was here that I embarked on a quest for knowledge, driven by a burning desire to unravel the mysteries of technology.

Becoming a Developer

As I ventured deeper into the realm of technology, my passion for development began to flourish. Armed with determination and fueled by a relentless drive for growth, I embarked on a journey of self-discovery. With each line of code I wrote and every algorithm I crafted, I found myself immersed in a world of endless possibilities. The challenges were daunting, but I faced them head-on, eager to unlock the secrets of software development. From mastering programming languages to exploring the intricacies of web development, I embraced every opportunity to expand my skill set. Late nights were spent poring over tutorials and documentation, each moment a testament to my unwavering dedication to the craft.

Nuclear Tech

As my journey through the realms of technology unfolded, I stumbled upon a fascination that would shape my path in unexpected ways: nuclear energy. Intrigued by its potential to revolutionize our world and intrigued by its complexities, I delved into the depths of nuclear science with fervor. With each discovery, from the intricacies of nuclear physics to the engineering marvels of nuclear reactors, I found myself drawn deeper into the world of atomic energy. It wasn't just the science that captivated me; it was the promise of a cleaner, more sustainable future powered by nuclear power.

Love forVolunteering

In the tapestry of my life, woven with threads of education and technological exploration, there exists a profound desire to make a difference beyond my personal pursuits. This chapter unfolds as I embrace the role of a volunteer, driven by a deep-seated commitment to serve my community and make a positive impact on the world around me. My journey as a volunteer began with a simple desire to give back—to share the blessings I've received and to uplift those in need. Whether it was through participating in local initiatives or joining hands with nonprofit organizations, I sought out opportunities to lend a helping hand wherever it was needed most.

Embracing myselfeveryday

As I journeyed through the tapestry of life, I discovered the power of self-love and acceptance. In a world that often seeks to define us by external standards and expectations, I learned to embrace my true self unapologetically. From my strengths to my flaws, my dreams to my fears, I accepted every part of myself with open arms. This journey of self-discovery was not without its challenges. I faced moments of doubt and insecurity, moments when I questioned my worth and my place in the world. But through it all, I held fast to the belief that I was enough, just as I was. And in that acceptance, I found a sense of peace and freedom that transcended the limitations of external validation.

Failures!!

Nah! Don't care. I've had my fair share of failures, but I don't let them define me. Instead, I see them as stepping stones to success. Each failure is a lesson learned, a chance to grow and evolve. So bring it on, world! I'm ready for whatever you throw my way.
